Conquer El Salvador's Highest Volcano & Relax by the Lake

Embark on a guided hike to the summit of Volcán Ilamatepec (Santa Ana Volcano), then unwind at the stunning turquoise waters of Lago Coatepeque, one of the most beautiful volcanic lakes in Central America.

Top 5 Highlights

1. Hike to the massive crater of Ilamatepec, with panoramic views of the surrounding valleys.

2. Witness the steaming sulfur lagoon inside the volcano’s crater.

3. Refresh at Lago Coatepeque – swim, kayak, or just relax on the lakeshore.

4. Scenic drive through coffee plantations and mountain villages.

5. Enjoy local food and fresh fish by the lake.
