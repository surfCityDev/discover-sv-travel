Our Story

DiscoverSVTravel was born out of a deep love for El Salvador and a passion for showing visitors the true heart of our beautiful country. From the beaches of Surf City to the highest mountain peaks and ancient Mayan ruins, we offer unforgettable journeys filled with culture, adventure, and authenticity.

Why Choose Us?

  • Local Expertise: We know El Salvador because we live it every day.
  • Bilingual Service: We speak both English and Spanish to better serve all travelers.
  • Custom Experiences: Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or culture—we tailor it for you.
  • Personal Connection: You're not just a tourist—you’re our guest.

Meet the Founder

Hi, I’m Byron Rogel—an El Salvadoran local with a dream of helping people fall in love with this country the way I did. With a background in tech and tourism, I combine digital convenience with warm hospitality to give you a smooth and memorable experience.
