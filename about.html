About Us

Who We Are

Discover-SV-Travel is led by Byron Rogel, a proud Salvadoran passionate about sharing the natural beauty, rich culture, and local secrets of El Salvador with travelers from around the world. Based in Surf City, La Libertad, we offer small-group, authentic tours designed to help visitors truly connect with our country beyond the typical tourist routes.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide meaningful, safe, and unforgettable experiences that help visitors discover the true essence of El Salvador — from its majestic volcanoes and colorful colonial towns to its world-famous beaches and rich cultural traditions.

Our Vision

We aim to become one of El Salvador’s leading tour providers by promoting sustainable tourism, supporting local communities, and showcasing the country's beauty through responsible and engaging travel.
