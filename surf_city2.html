Surf City 2 – Eastern Beaches & Scenic Views

Explore El Salvador’s newest coastal route linking Punta Mango, Las Flores, El Cuco, and beyond. Experience breathtaking viewpoints, modern bike paths, and pristine surf spots along a 13 km scenic corridor.

Top 5 Highlights

1. Drive along the brand-new dual carriageway with coastal panoramas.

2. Discover world-class surf at Punta Mango and Las Flores.

3. Enjoy modern scenic viewpoints perfect for photography.

4. Relax on quiet, golden-sand beaches like El Cuco and El Toro.

5. Experience local cuisine at roadside seafood spots.
