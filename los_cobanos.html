Explore El Salvador’s Only Coral Reef

Snorkel through colorful marine life, walk along volcanic rock formations, and relax on tranquil beaches at Los Cóbanos – a hidden gem on El Salvador’s Pacific coast.

Top 5 Highlights

1. Guided snorkeling adventure at the largest coral reef in Central America.

2. Fresh seafood lunch by the beach (optional all-inclusive).

3. Relax on volcanic sand beaches away from crowds.

4. Watch local fishermen and spot dolphins or whales (seasonal).

5. Perfect blend of marine life, nature, and coastal culture.
