El Tunco + Tamanique Waterfalls Adventure

Discover the perfect blend of beach and jungle! This tour takes you to:

  1. Guided hike to stunning Tamanique Waterfalls (with optional cliff jumping & swimming)
  2. Seafood lunch at El Tunco beach (optional, all-inclusive option)
  3. Relax on the black sand beach & explore local surf shops
  4. Enjoy breathtaking sunset views over the Pacific Ocean
  5. Includes transport, guide, bottled water & entrance fees

Price: $70 with lunch / $50 without lunch

Perfect for: Adventure seekers, nature lovers, and beach fans
