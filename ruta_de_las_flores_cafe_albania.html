Adventure & Culture Amid Coffee Farms

Visit Café Albania in Apaneca—where adrenaline meets coffee. Enjoy bike and surfboard zip‑lines, the giant rainbow slide, a butterfly swing, and lush coffee gardens across the famous Ruta de Las Flores. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

1. Ride the **320 m Rainbow Slide**—one of El Salvador’s largest rainbow slides. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

2. Experience the thrill of the **bike zip‑line 30 m high** over coffee fields. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}

3. Brave the **surfboard zip‑line** and **giant butterfly swing** for more fun. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}

4. Get lost in the **largest hedge maze in Central America** for a family adventure. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}

5. Sample fresh, locally sourced coffee and lunch in charming Apaneca. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}

6. Walk around colorful Apaneca & Ataco murals and artisan shops. :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}

7. Easy transport—about 1.5–2 h from San Salvador or coastal towns. :contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}
