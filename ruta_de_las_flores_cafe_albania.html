Adventure & Culture Amid Coffee Farms

Visit Café Albania in Apaneca—where adrenaline meets coffee. Enjoy bike and surfboard zip‑lines, the giant rainbow slide, a butterfly swing, and lush coffee gardens across the famous Ruta de Las Flores.

Top 7 Highlights

1. Ride the 320 m Rainbow Slide one of El Salvador’s largest rainbow slides.

2. Experience the thrill of the bike zip‑line 30 m high over coffee fields.

3. Brave the surfboard zip‑line and giant butterfly swing for more fun.

4. Get lost in the largest hedge maze in Central America for a family adventure.

5. Sample fresh, locally sourced coffee and lunch in charming Apaneca.

6. Walk around colorful Apaneca & Ataco murals and artisan shops.

7. Easy transport—about 1.5–2 h from San Salvador or coastal towns.

Price Extras: Variable depending on activities (e.g., Rainbow Slide $10, Bike Zip‑Line $10, Maze $3, big swing $5)

Includes: Guide, transportation (if booked through a tour), entry, coffee tastings, parking, bathrooms.
