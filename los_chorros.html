Cool Off at One of El Salvador’s Most Popular Natural Pools

Discover Los Chorros, a series of crystal-clear waterfalls and natural swimming pools nestled in lush tropical scenery – perfect for families and travelers seeking a refreshing escape.

Top 5 Highlights

1. Swim in fresh, spring-fed natural pools surrounded by volcanic rock walls.

2. Relax under the cascading falls and listen to the sound of rushing water.

3. Enjoy a picnic in shaded areas near the pools.

4. Short, easy walking trails through green scenery.

5. Family-friendly spot close to San Salvador.
